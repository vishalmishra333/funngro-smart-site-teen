FAQ

Questions teens often ask.

Is it legal for teenagers to work with companies on Funngro?

Teens aged 14+ can work with companies after sharing age proof and parent approval. Projects are structured so that you learn, earn and stay safe at the same time.

Do I need to be an expert before applying to projects?

No. There are projects for beginners and for experts. Start with simple tasks, join learning programs inside the app and keep leveling up your profile.

How will I get paid for my work?

Once your work is approved, money is credited to your Funngro wallet. You can withdraw it to your bank account or UPI as per the app guidelines.
